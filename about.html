Howdy!

My name is Santiago Garcia, but most of my friends call me Santi. I'm a student at Texas A&M University majoring in Industrial and Systems Engineering and Biochemistry with a strong interest in Product Development, Front-End Development, Research and Development, and in making people happy.

When I'm not studying, spending time with my family, or hanging out with friends, I love to play music. I began playing piano when I was 10 and then picked up the violin one year later when I joined the school orchestra. I've kept up with both instruments since and during stressful times they are my best way of taking a break and enjoying the moment.

As a child of Colombian immigrants, I have learned about the power of dedication and dreaming.
